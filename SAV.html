Formulae of Surface Area and Volume

1.    Cuboid

Length of the cuboid = L
width of the cuboid = B
heigth of the box = H
Total Surface area of cuboid = 2( L.B + B.H + H.L )
Curve surface area of volume = 2( L.B + B.H )
Volume of the cuboid = L x B x H

2.    Cube

In cube length , width and height all are equal (i.e. L = B = H ) .
length of the side of cube = L
Total Surface area of cube = 6L2
Curve surface area of cube = 4L2
Volume of the cube = L3

3.    Cylender

Radius of the cylender = r
Height of the cylender = H
Total Surface area of cylender = 2πr( r + H )
Curve surface area of cylender = 2πrH
Volume of the cylender = πr2H

4.    Cone

Radius of the cone = r
Height of the cone = H
slant height of cone = L
slant height ( L ) = √( r2 + H2 )
Total Surface area of cone = πr( L + r )
Curve surface area of cone = πrL
Volume of the cone = πr2H / 3

5.    Frustum

Smaller Radius of the frustum = r
Larger Radius of the frustum = R
Height of the Frustum = h
slant height of frustum = L
slant height ( L ) = √( ( R - r )2 + H2 )
Surface area of cone = π( R + r )√( ( R - r )2 + H2 )
Volume of the cone = πr2H / 3
