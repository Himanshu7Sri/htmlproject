Introduction to Surface Area and Volume

Suppose , there was a cuboidal shape box on my roof and if it was raining yesterday night and the box is completely filled with water and if i need to know how much water is there in the box so , i need to calculate the volume of that box that tells me how much water is there in the box

length of the box = L
breadth or width of the box = B
height of the box = H

Volume of the box = L x B x H


and suppose if i want to paint this box from all of its outer surface so , i need to calculate the total surface area of the box i.e.

length of the box = L
breadth or width of the box = B
height of the box = H

Total surface area of the box = 2( L.B + B.H + H.L )


suppose you are in a room and you have a contract to paint that room so you need to calculate the area of of the four wall and area of the top most wall that is
length of the wall = L
width of the wall = B
height of the wall = H

Area that needs to be painted = 2( B.H + H.L ) + L.B
