Introduction to the Polynomial

Identity - identity is an equality which is true for all values of a variable .
Degree of a polynomial - highest power of a variable in a polynomial is known as degree of polynomial .
Note - the degree of a non zero or constant polynomial is zero .

Example - find the degree in the followings -
1. x + x2 + 56 ......................... degree => 2
2. 2x5 + 3x8 + x2 + 56 ...............degree => 8
3. 2x4 + 3x3 + x2 + 1 ...............degree => 4

Linear Polynomial - A polynomial of degree 1 is known as linear polynomial .
Example - ax + b => degree = 1
Quadratic Polynomial - A polynomial of degree 2 is known as Quadratic polynomial .
Example - ax2 + b => degree = 2
Cubic Polynomial - A polynomial of degree 3 is known as cubic polynomial .
Example - ax3 + bx2 + c => degree = 3
