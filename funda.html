Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic

Theorem : Every composite number can be expressed as a product of primes , and his factorisation is unique , apart from the order in which the prime factor occur .
Note - The prime factorisation of a natural number is unique , except for the order of its factor .

         12600 = 2 x 2 x 2 x 3 x 3 x 5 x 5 x 7
         12600 = 23 x 32 x 52 x 7

HCF - Product of the smallest power of each common prime factor in the number .
LCM - Product of the greatest power of each prime factor involved in the number .

Relation between HCF and LCM -
          HCF( a , b ) x LCM( a , b ) = a x b
