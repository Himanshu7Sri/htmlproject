Introduction to Pair of Linear Equation in Two Variable

let us first understand what this chapter means -

since linear equation means a polynomial equation with degree one ( i.e. equation of the type ax + b ... ) . linear equation in two variable means linear equation with any two variable ( i.e. equation of type ax + by + c ... ) and here name of the chapter comes with pair of that means two equation with two variables

Example - a1x + b1y + c1 = 0
                   a2x + b2y + c2 = 0
where a1 ≠ 0 , a2 ≠ 0 , b1 ≠ 0 , b2 ≠ 0 , c1 , c2 are some constant value
x , y are the variables
This Example is also the general form of linear equation in two variable

Example - 5x + 4y = 10
                  3x + 4y = 6
