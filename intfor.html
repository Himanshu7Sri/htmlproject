Internal Forces

When you calculate the force using F = ma that time you calculate the total force -
∑F is the sum of all the force acting on the system and this will be the vector addition addition of the force .
∑m is the total mass of all the bodies in the system .
acm is the acceleration of the center of mass of all the bodies in the system .

∑F = (∑m)(acm)




force equation for body A is -
F - FBA = mAaA


force equation for body B is -
FAB + F = mBaB


here FAB & FBA both are equal because these are action and reaction forces so when we add both the equation we got

F - FBA + FAB + F = mAaA + mBaB
2F = mAaA + mBaB
F = (mAaA + mBaB)/2
