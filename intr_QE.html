Introduction to Quadratic Equations

Quadratic equation is a type of a polynomial of degree two . Quadratic polynomial is of the form of --
                      ax2 + bx + c ,     where a ≠ 0

when we equate this polynomial to zero then this quadratic polynomial is converted to quadratic equation .
              ax2 + bx + c = 0,     where a ≠ 0

After simplify if equation are in the form of ax2 + bx + c = 0 that means equation are quadratic equation
