Algebric Method

Substitution Method - the value of one variable by expressing it in term of the other variable .
Let us understand this concept by an example -

Example - solve the following linear equation using substittution method -
                2x + y = 6
                4x + 3y = 4
Solution -
Step 1 : pick any of the two equation and find the value of one variable in term of another here i pick the first equation
                2x + y = 6
                y = 6 - 2x

Step 2 : now substitute the value of y in the other( second ) equation -
                4x + 3y = 4
                4x + 3(6 - 2x) = 4
                4x + 18 - 6x = 4
                18 - 2x = 4
                2x = 18 - 4
                2x = 14
               => x = 7
Step 3 : now put the value of x in any of the two equation and get the value of y
                2x + y = 6
                2 x 7 + y = 6
                y = 6 - 14
               => y = -8


Elimination Method - In this method we eliminate one variable and find the value of the other variable this is more convenient method than substitution .
Let's solve one question to more understand this method -

Example - solve the following linear equation using elimination method -
                2x + 3y = 5
                3x + 2y = 10
