Introduction to Coordinate Geometry

Why we need Cartesian Plane ?
To find the position of an object , to measure the exact distance between any two objects from a given fixed position . the given fixed point are most of the time is origin .



X - Axis - The Horizontal line in the cartesian plane is known as X - axis it is also known as abscissa .
Y - Axis - The Vertical line in the cartesian plane is known as Y - axis it is also known as Ordinate .
Origin - The fixed point in the cartesian plane is known as Origin . it is zero distance from both the abscissa and ordinate so , it's co-ordinate is ( 0 , 0 )
Co-ordinates - it is the point in the form of ( x , y ) which tell us how far we are from origin . abscissa .
