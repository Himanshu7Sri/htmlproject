Introduction to Circles

Circle : Locus of a point which moves in a plane in such a way that its distance from a fixed point remains constant ( in the same given plane ) .
                      OR
         A circle is a collection of all the point in a plane in such a way that it's distance from a fixed point remains constant ( in the same given plane ) .

Centre of circle : that fixed point is known as centre of a circle .
Radius of circle : that constant distance is known as radius of the circle .
