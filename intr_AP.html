Introduction to Arithmetic Progression

as we seen that in our daily life not all but some of the things follows some sequence ..... that sequence may be a table of any number ( it also follow a sequence with some common difference ) , pocket money of a boy / girl that increases some fixed amount in some fixed duration or anything like that .

Sequence : A succession of terms a1 , a2 , a3 , a4 ....... formed according to some rule or law , known as sequence .

Sequences are of two types -
1 . Finite Sequence
2 . Infinite Sequence

Finite Sequence : A sequence that have a finite number of terms or limited terms , known as finite Sequence .
Infinite Sequence : A sequence that have not finite number of terms , known as infinite Sequence .

Example : 1 , 4 , 9 , 16 ...... , 100                              => Finite Sequence
                  1 , 1 , 2 , 3 , 5 , 8 .......                              => Infinite Sequence
                  1 , 4 , 7 , 10 , 13 .......                               => Infinite Sequence
                  1/2 , 1/5 , 1/10 , 1/17 ......                         => Infinite Sequence .
                  1/1 , 1/2 , 1/4 , 1/8 , 1/16 ...... , 1/2048     => Finite Sequence
